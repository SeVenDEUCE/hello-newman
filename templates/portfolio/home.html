Newman Studios and Fine Art is an illustration, design and fine art studio located in Charlotte, North Carolina. Owner, Greg Newman, is a recognized freelance artist specializing in portrait, wildlife, motorcyle and technical paintings and illustrations. Greg also works as a devigner (designer & developer) servicing clients that include (but are not limited to) National Geographic, Discovery Channel and Broadway.
